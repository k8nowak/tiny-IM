
--- Lesson Headers ---


Unit 5

Functions

Lesson 16

Finding and Interpreting Inverse Functions





--- What Students Do ---


In pairs, exchange information to identify and evaluate inverse functions related to various scenarios, such as cost and temperature conversions.


--- What Students Learn ---


(1) Understand the concept of an inverse function in real-world contexts.  
(2) Find the inverse of a linear function by rearranging the equation.


--- Where It Fits ---


**a deepening of understanding of inverse functions through real-world applications; practicing equations for multiple operations.**
