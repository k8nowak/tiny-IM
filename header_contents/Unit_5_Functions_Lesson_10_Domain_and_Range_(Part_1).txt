
--- Lesson Headers ---


Unit 5

Functions

Lesson 10

Domain and Range (Part 1)





--- What Students Do ---


In pairs, identify the domain and range of various functions based on real-world contexts by categorizing possible and impossible input values, and reasoning through their characteristics.


--- What Students Learn ---


(1) to identify and determine the reasonable domain of a function (2) to identify and determine the reasonable range of a function (3) to understand the definitions of domain and range in the context of functions.


--- Where It Fits ---


**an exploration of defining input and output values for functions; understanding domain and range in context**
