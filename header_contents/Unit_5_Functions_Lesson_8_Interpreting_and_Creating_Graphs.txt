Lesson 8

Interpreting and Creating Graphs





--- What Students Do ---


In pairs, interpret and create graphs representing the height of a flag being raised over time and describe their features in the context of the situation.


--- What Students Learn ---


(1) interpreting the average rate of change in a situation  
(2) identifying and explaining key features of graphs  
(3) sketching graphs to represent verbal or visual situations


--- Where It Fits ---


interpreting complex situations through graphical representations; fostering mathematical modeling and problem-solving skills.
